About The Movie

Hello! My name is Tyler Sauls, I'm a freshman at NYU Abu Dhabi planning to major in Computer Science. The inspiration behind the movie comes from the funny and relatable experiences my friends and I had during our first few weeks on campus. I wanted to capture those moments, and that is how my movie was created!
